 At the final act in 1817, Pushkin also read a poem "Faith". But Pushkin did not vanish in this diversity, he was looking for.
Soon Pushkin moved to Petersburg and enrolled in the College of foreign Affairs in the rank of collegiate Secretary.In 1815, Pushkin read triumph in the exam his poem "Recollection in Tsarskoe Selo" in the presence of the famous poet Derzhavin: "old Man Derzhavin noticed us and in a coffin descending, has blessed". His communication is very wide: the hussars, the poets, the literary society "Arzamas" and "Green lamp", theatres, trendy restaurants, a duel – "thank God it's not deadly," as reported by Karamzin, Vyazemsky his brother.
